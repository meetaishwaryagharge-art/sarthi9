Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, completely. We offer a free 30-minute financial review with no obligation to buy anything. We believe you should understand your options before making any decision.

Can we meet in person?

Absolutely. Our office is in Thane (West). We also offer phone and video consultations for clients across Mumbai and Maharashtra who prefer not to travel.

I already have insurance. Can you review it?

Yes — in fact, many clients come to us for a second opinion. We'll review your existing policies and tell you honestly whether they serve your needs or not.

How quickly will you respond?

We respond to all enquiries within 4 business hours. WhatsApp messages are usually answered the same day, including on Saturdays.

Will my information be shared?

Never. Everything you share with us is completely confidential and used solely to advise you. We do not sell or share client data with any third party.

Can you help with existing claims?

Yes. Claim support is one of our core services. Even if you didn't buy your policy through us, we can often help guide you through the claim process.
