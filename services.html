What Should You Know?

Many people are under-insured or in the wrong product. Here's what we address in every life insurance consultation:

  • How much cover do I actually need?Typically 15–20× your annual income, considering debts and dependents.
  • Term vs Endowment vs ULIP — which is right?Depends on your age, income, and goals. We explain all options clearly.
  • What happens if I miss a premium?Grace periods, lapse rules, and revival options vary — we guide you.
  • How do I make a claim?We assist your family through the entire claim process — documentation and follow-up.
  • Can I add riders?Critical illness, accidental disability, waiver of premium — we advise the right add-ons.

What Should You Know?

Medical costs in India are rising at 14% annually. A good health plan is now essential, not optional:

  • Family floater vs individual — which is better?Depends on your family's age, health history, and usage pattern.
  • What's a waiting period?Pre-existing conditions usually have 2–4 year waits — buy early for full benefit.
  • What's not covered?Cosmetic surgery, dental, and certain treatments — we explain exclusions clearly.
  • How does cashless hospitalisation work?We help you choose plans with strong network hospitals near your home.
  • Do I need a top-up plan?Super top-ups offer high cover at low cost — often the smartest add-on.

What Should You Know?

Mutual funds are for everyone — not just the wealthy or financially savvy. Key guidance we provide:

  • How much should I start with?SIPs can begin at ₹500/month. We size your investment to your income and goal.
  • Which funds are best right now?We focus on long-term fit over short-term performance rankings.
  • What about market crashes?SIP investors actually benefit from corrections. We coach you to stay disciplined.
  • When should I sell?When your goal is met — not when markets fall. We guide this decision.
  • Are my returns taxable?LTCG and STCG rules apply — we advise on tax-efficient withdrawal timing.

Key Add-ons We Advise

The right add-ons can dramatically reduce out-of-pocket costs during a claim. We guide you through the best options:

  • Zero Depreciation CoverClaim full repair cost without depreciation deduction — worth every rupee for new vehicles.
  • Engine & Gearbox ProtectionCovers damage from water ingression and mechanical failure — essential in monsoon-heavy regions.

What Does Property Insurance Cover?

Many people assume property insurance is only for commercial buildings. Here's why homeowners need it too:

  • Fire & related perilsFire, explosion, lightning, and implosion — the primary covered risks for structure and contents.
  • Natural calamitiesFlood, cyclone, earthquake — essential in Maharashtra's coastal and flood-prone zones.
  • Burglary & theftContents cover protects electronics, jewellery, and valuables stored at home.
  • Structural damageProtects the physical structure of your home from listed perils.
  • Business premises coverStock, machinery, and premises for shops and small businesses.
