Our Journey

Built on Trust,
Grown Through Relationships

Suketu Sarthi was founded with a simple belief: every family deserves an honest, knowledgeable guide for their financial life. Not just someone who sells policies — but someone who truly understands your life, your goals, and your worries.

What started as a local advisory practice in Thane has grown over three decades into a multi-service wealth and insurance firm, serving clients across generations. Many of our clients today are the children and grandchildren of our earliest policyholders.

We are IRDAI-registered and AMFI-registered advisors with access to plans from all major insurers and fund houses — ensuring our recommendations are always in your interest, not ours.
